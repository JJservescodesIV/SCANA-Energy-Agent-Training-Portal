Fixed-Rate Plan

Stability & Predictability

✅ Pros:
  • Budget certainty
  • Protection from spikes
  • Peace of mind
❌ Cons:
  • May miss low prices
  • Contract commitment
  • Early termination fees

✅ Pros:
  • Benefit from dips
  • No commitment
  • Switch anytime

  • Bill uncertainty
  • Winter price spikes
  • Budget challenges
